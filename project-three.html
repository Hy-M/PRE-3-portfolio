Moments app & digital picture frame

In my final two weeks at Northcoders, I was in a team with five other students and we were left to create our own project from scratch in just eight days.

Moments features a mobile app which allows the user to sign up and upload a selection of their favourite photos taken from their camera or camera roll to be displayed onto a Raspberry Pi-controlled digital picture frame. The picture frame also makes use of facial detection and facial recognition to allow a usersâ€™ pictures to be displayed without needing to input their credentials again.
